Tips for the best photo-taking experience possible

  1. NEVER look directly at the sun when totality is not yet reached
  2. Practice camera settings before the day of the eclipse, so you know what to expect
  3. During the period of totality, feel free to look at the corona with your eyes!
  4. Happy sighting!
