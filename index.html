Why I chose research on Solar Eclipses

On April 8, 2024 me and my dad went all the way down to Canada which ended up being about a six hour drive in order to witness totality. I was looking forward to this the whole week, and it definitely did not disappoint as I was able to see the corona for a solid three and a half minutes before the sun finally was shown by the moon. But this experience was so awesome, I wanted to learn more about how they work which is why I'm here.

Recap: Comparison of Solar Eclipse Types

Eclipse Type Description Can You See the Corona?
Total The Moon completely blocks the Sun. Yes
Partial The Moon blocks only part of the Sun. No
Annular The Moon appears smaller than the Sun, leaving a bright ring. No
