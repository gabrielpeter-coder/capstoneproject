What is a Solar Eclipse?

A solar eclipse happens when the Moon moves directly between the Earth and the Sun. The Moon blocks some or all of the Sun's light, causing the sky to become darker for a short period of time.

Types of Solar Eclipses

  • Total Eclipse
  • Partial Eclipse
  • Hybrid Eclipse

Total Eclipse: when the moon fully masks the sun

Partial Eclipse: when the moon somewhat masks the sun

Hybrid Eclipse: Sun changes from a "Ring of Fire" to a total eclipse, a super rare event
